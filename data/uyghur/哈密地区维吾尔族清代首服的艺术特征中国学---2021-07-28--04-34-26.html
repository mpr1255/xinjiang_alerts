    哈密地区维吾尔族清代首服的艺术特征

    Costume Artistic Features of Uygur Nationality during the Qing Dynasty in Hami Area

    【作者】 许仲林； 邬红芳；

    【Author】 XU Zhong-lin;WU Hong-fang;School of Artic and Design,Suzhou University;

    【机构】 宿州学院美术与设计学院；

    【摘要】 对清代哈密地区维吾尔族女性首服的独特艺术特征进行探究,女子戴头巾、帽子和男子戴帽子的习俗有其客观自然环境、地理位置因素,和民族的标志性观念因素、传统道德标准因素、民族的宗教信仰与禁忌因素以及民族认同感等因素有密切的关联性,穿戴首服的这些渊源的历史特征,反映出清朝时期其民族的独特民俗文化和社会文化现象以及维吾尔族女性的社会角色。更多还原

    【Abstract】 The Uygur women costume in Hami area during the Qing Dynasty was analyzed for its own unique artistic features,the woman wearing a scarf,hat and a man wearing a hat custom have its objective factors of natural environment,geographical location,and the national symbol of the concept,the traditional moral standard factors,ethnic religion and taboo factors and national identity factors have close correlation,the historical characteristics during Qing Dynasty in Hami Uygur dress headgear these origins,the characteristics reflected the nation unique folk culture,social and cultural phenomenon,the social role of Uygur women.更多还原

    【关键词】 维吾尔族； 清代首服； 艺术特征；
    【Key words】 Uygur Nationality； Qing Dynasty costume； artistic characteristics；
