新疆哈密地区维吾尔族女性传统服饰及其文化特征

【摘要】 新疆地区自古就有诸多少数民族聚居生活,因地理位置的关系,多种思想、文化、宗教在此交汇融合。在民族发展融合过程中,各种文明不断地相互碰撞、相互借鉴,逐渐形成了具有地域性特质的文化特征和艺术特征。作为东疆服饰艺术的代表,哈密地区维吾尔族的传统服饰吸纳了汉、满、蒙文化的元素,服饰文化特征与其他地区同民族间存在显著不同,这种文化充分体现出该地区的西域与中原文化的交叠,这也是哈密地区维吾尔族的民族特征、宗教、文化的印记。更多还原

【Abstract】 There were many minorities living in xinjiang province from ancient time,a variety of thoughts,cultures and religions interact there.In the procedure of nationality development,different cultures interact and learn from each other,they gradually formed some cultural and artistic characteristics with geographical peculiarity.As the representative of eastern xinjiang province, the traditional clothing of HAMI district absorbs the elements of han, man and meng people, the cultural characteristics of clothing of this district is unlike that of other districts of the same nationality,this kind of culture is not only sufficient to reflect the overlap between the western area of china and the central area of china,but also represent the stamps of nationality characteristics,religion and culture of HAMI district.更多还原

【Key words】 Hami district； Uyghur nationality； The traditional clothing of female； cultural characteristics；
